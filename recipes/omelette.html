Basic Omelette

Description

What can you really say that would make this omelette stand out from the rest? Nothing, really. It's just a basic omelette with maybe some extra spices. Though, I don't really add milk to mine, mostly 'cause my stomache would hate me for it.

I'm pretty basic, and that's quite alright with me

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil (could replace with meat fat from cooking)
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • spices (optional)
  • other add-ins like spinach (optional)
  1. Crack open the eggs.
  2. Add your salt and pepper to taste and any other spice(s) you're feeling at the time.
  3. (Optional) Add your leafy green veg here so they don't completely desintigrate in the pan.
  4. Heat pan to medium heat.
  5. (Optional) Cook your chopped meat, like bacon, leaving everything in the pan.
  6. Add your eggs (just after your meat is done, if added) and cook until you have a pancake looking solid (about 3 minutes), then flip carefully to finish cooking.
  7. Plate up, add your favorite sauce (I like Chipotle Tabasco!), and enjoy!
