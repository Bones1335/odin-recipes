Basic Stir Fry (with a twist!)

Description

Who doesn't love a good Stir Fry? It's one of the easiest things to make, and generally only takes about 10 minutes to throw together. Therefore, a fantastic go to meal at the end of a long work day (hopefully you have rice premade or a quick way to make some).

However, let's spice things up a little (pun intended, 100%). How about adding some beans to the party? I know, seems weird. But trust me, it's worth it!

please note: there are no measurements because I don't measure anything in this recipe as I just eye-ball as I go. Not the smartest move for everybody, but it works for me.

Ingredients

  • chopped broccoli
  • chopped carrots
  • 1 can of red beans
  • neutral oil
  • sesame oil
  • rice vinegar
  • fish sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce (don't judge!)
  1. Heat pan to medium-high heat and add your neutral oil.
  2. Throw in your carrots and broccoli, making sure not to stir too often (kind of an ironic concept, right?).
  3. After about 5 minutes, add in your beans along with your liquids to taste, cooking for an additional 5 minutes, or so.
  4. Plate up over a bed of rice and enjoy!
